Prior to this analysis, risk reporting was fragmented across multiple departments, leading to:

* **Lagging Indicators:**
Difficulties in identifying "stress zones" in the CRE sector before delinquency occurred.

* **Concentration Blind Spots:**
A lack of granular visibility into the risk profile of specific metros like Killeen, TX.

* **Missed Retention Opportunities:**
Inability to efficiently identify low-risk customers for the "Safe Balance" outreach program.
